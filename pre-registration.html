Admission of applicants for the 2022-2023 academic year in the ACT

Admission of applicants for the 2022-2023 academic year in the secondary educational institution of Alatoo International University will be held in an ofline format. To pass the admission ezam to the secondary educational institution, the applicant must pay attention to the following points:

  • Choice of the profession:
    • Economics and accounting (accountant);
    • Marketing (marketer);
    • Computer systems and complexes-Information technologies and systems (technician);
    • Design (computer graphic) (designer);
    • Software for computers and automated systems (technician-programmer);
  • Registration for the exam
    • Online registration for the exam (click here)
    • Login system, login, and passwords are sent to the applicant’s personal email.
  • Examination Content
    The exam consists of two parts: 100 questions in English, 50 questions in mathematics, in the form of tests and classics.
    • English
      • Grammar
      • Vocabulary
      • reading comprehension
    • Mathematics
      • Mathematics
      • Logic
  • Exam date
  • at 08:00 Registration of a participant in the entrance test to IT & Business College (at the AMU).
    • Exams will be held:
      • June 4, 2022, from 09:00 to 11:00 (exam time 120 minutes), in ENGLISH;
      • June 4, 2022, from 11:30 to 12:30 (exam time 60 minutes), in the subject of MATH.
    • The applicant should have only a pencil & pen and self-confidence.
